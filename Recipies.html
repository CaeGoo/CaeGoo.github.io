Pancakes
Ingredient Quantity
Flour 2 cups
Milk 1.5 cups
Eggs 2 large
Sugar 2 tablespoons
Baking Powder 1 tablespoon

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix flour, sugar, and baking powder.
  2. Add milk and eggs, and whisk until smooth.
  3. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat.
  4. Pour batter onto the skillet and cook until bubbles form.
  5. Flip and cook until golden brown.
  6. Serve with syrup or toppings of your choice.
Chicken Curry
Ingredient Quantity
Chicken 1 kg
Curry Powder 2 tablespoons
Coconut Milk 400 ml
Onion 1 large, chopped
Garlic 3 cloves, minced

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, sauté onion, garlic, and ginger until fragrant.
  2. Add chicken and cook until browned.
  3. Stir in curry powder and cook for another minute.
  4. Add coconut milk and simmer until chicken is cooked through.
  5. Serve with rice or naan.
Spaghetti Bolognese
Ingredient Quantity
Spaghetti 400 g
Ground Beef 500 g
Tomato Sauce 400 g
Onion 1 large, chopped
Garlic 2 cloves, minced

Instructions

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. In a pan, sauté onion and garlic in olive oil.
  3. Add ground beef and cook until browned.
  4. Stir in tomato sauce and simmer for 15 minutes.
  5. Serve over spaghetti.
