Featured Recipes

Pancakes

Today, pancakes are a globally popular dish, especially in Western countries where they are a staple breakfast food.The earliest known pancakes date back to Ancient Greece and Rome. They are thin, flat cakes typically made from a batter of flour, eggs, milk, and a leavening agent like baking powder. Cooked on a griddle or frying pan, they are golden brown on both sides and soft inside. Pancakes are often served for breakfast and can be topped with syrup, butter, fruit, or other sweet or savory toppings. Different cultures have their own versions.

Spaghetti Bolognese

Spaghetti Bolognese is a classic Italian pasta dish made with a rich meat sauce. The sauce typically includes ground beef, tomatoes, onions, garlic, and herbs like basil and oregano. It is simmered to develop deep flavors and served over cooked spaghetti. This hearty dish is often garnished with grated Parmesan cheese and fresh basil.The dish gained widespread popularity in the 20th century, especially in countries like the UK, the U.S., and Australia.

Chicken Curry

Today, chicken curry is enjoyed globally, with popular versions including Indian butter chicken, Thai green curry, Japanese curry, and Caribbean-style curry chicken.The dish has ancient roots in the Indian subcontinent, where curries have been a staple for thousands of years. Chicken curry is a flavorful dish made with chicken pieces cooked in a spiced sauce. The sauce often includes ingredients like onions, garlic, ginger, tomatoes, and a blend of spices such as cumin, coriander, turmeric, and chili powder. It can be creamy or tomato-based and is typically served with rice or bread. Chicken curry is popular in many cuisines, including Indian, Thai, and Caribbean.
